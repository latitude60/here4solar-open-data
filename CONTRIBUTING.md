# Corrections and contributions

Corrections are welcome when they can be checked against a primary source.

Please provide:

- the supplier or legal licensee;
- the affected tariff or record;
- the exact field that should change;
- a public supplier or Ofgem URL supporting the change;
- the date the source was checked;
- a short explanation of the correction.

Secondary comparison tables can help locate a possible change, but they are not sufficient evidence for a rate or eligibility update by themselves.

Do not submit customer account details, application documents, meter identifiers or other personal information.

For a simple correction without a repository workflow, use the [Here4Solar contact page](https://here4solar.uk/contact-us/).
